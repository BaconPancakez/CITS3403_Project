What is UUUA?

UUUA is a community platform built by UWA students, for UWA students. Share your study notes, discover resources uploaded by your peers, and leave honest reviews and feedback on the units you have studied — all in one place.

📄
Upload Notes

Share your summaries, lecture notes, and study guides with fellow UWA students to help everyone succeed.

🔍
Find Resources

Browse notes and materials uploaded by other students across a wide range of UWA units and faculties.

⭐
Review Courses

Leave ratings and written feedback on the units you have completed to guide students choosing their next enrolment.

How it works

1
Create an account

Register with your UWA email address — students and staff only.

2
Browse or upload

Search for your unit and download notes, or contribute your own to the community.

3
Rate and review

Share your experience of a unit to help future students make informed decisions.
